**The school community at Sir Apollo Kaggwa Primary School Nakasero came alive today as learners enthusiastically participated in the open campaigns ahead of the School General Elections, scheduled for tomorrow, Friday, 13th March 2026. The event provided a vibrant platform for aspiring young leaders to present their ideas, share their visions, and engage their peers in meaningful dialogue about leadership and service.**

![](/img/1001516790.jpg)

**From the outset, the candidates impressed with their creativity, confidence, and sense of responsibility. Each learner outlined clear plans to enhance school life, highlighting the importance of unity, cooperation, and active participation. Their speeches reflected not only personal ambition but also a genuine commitment to serving the school community.**

**Central to this year’s elections is the inspiring theme: “Empowering Voices, Building Leaders.”. This theme underscores the school’s dedication to giving every learner the opportunity to be heard, express their ideas freely, and develop the skills necessary to become responsible and visionary leaders of tomorrow. It serves as a guiding principle for nurturing leadership, confidence, and critical thinking among our pupils.**

![](/img/1001516792.jpg)

**These campaigns are also an essential part of our commitment to holistic education. At Sir Apollo Kaggwa Primary School Nakasero, we focus not only on academic excellence but also on talent development and leadership growth. By integrating these elements, we ensure that every learner is equipped to excel academically, socially, and personally.**

**Beyond leadership skills, the day’s activities offered a practical lesson in democracy, accountability, and respectful dialogue. By listening to and evaluating their peers’ proposals, learners gained hands-on experience in decision-making, understanding the qualities needed to choose leaders who can represent them effectively.**

![](/img/1001516798.jpg)

**Meanwhile, all preparations for the elections have been finalized, with voting materials organized to guarantee a smooth and orderly process tomorrow. Learners are eager to participate, putting into practice the values of leadership and democracy they have been learning about throughout the campaigns.**

**The energy, enthusiasm, and leadership potential on display today reflect the vibrant spirit of our school community. Our learners’ active involvement demonstrates their readiness to take responsibility, contribute meaningfully, and grow as leaders with integrity and vision.**
